Air Jordan 1 High OG « Hyper Royal »

A new icy-hued Air Jordan 1 High arrives this April.

The Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG Hyper Royal is already a spring 2021 must-have. After the University Blue few weeks ago, the Hyper Royal Jordan 1 mixes white, gray and sky blue with a incredible marbled effect. The shoes are made of a smooth leather base associated with a quality nubuck. This new Jordan 1 will arrive on April 17, 2021 on Nike.com and at select Jordan Brand retailers, priced at 160€. Good cop!

Kim Jones x Converse

The artistic director of Dior homme takes possession of the legendary Chuck Tailor 70.

By working on the Chuck 70 shoes, Kim Jones brings the Converse classic back in the wake of his 2021 creativity. The sneakers will be available in two colors: black & white. This Kim Jones x Converse Chuck 70 will be released on April 8 on Converse.com and at select retailers. Its price is 160€, so cop or drop?

Adidas Yeezy 700 V3 Kyanite

The Yeezy 700 V3 come back in a new color.

This Yeezy is a UFO in the sneaker landscape. Its atypical construction reflects the signature of the genius of Kanye West. The adidas Yeezy 700 V3 “Kyanite” will arrive on May 27 on Adidas.fr and at selected retailers, for the usual price of 200€. Stay connected!
